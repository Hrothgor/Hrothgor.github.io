About this project

This project is my first gameJam. We were a group of 4 developers and we had 2 days to make the game.

The theme of the gameJam was "Past, future, present", and we were forced to have quotes in the game.

No one in the group had ever used unity. At the beginning it was a little difficult to find oneself in the editor because we had never used an engine in the genre.

We had decided to make a 2D isometric puzzle game. Isometric 2D was new to us. But finally we managed to have a finished game.
